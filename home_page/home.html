App Overview

Introducing our app, designed to ensure you never miss out on exciting events in Vancouver, from concerts and festivals to cultural celebrations. Stay updated on limited-time discounts, including in-store sales and weekly promotions, so you can take advantage of every deal. Plus, discover hidden gems around the city, from one-of-a-kind stores to unique tourist spots you might have otherwise overlooked.

Our Logo

The PREMIERE logo is designed to symbolize a ticket with a bar code, which also doubles as a representation of a bridge. This bridge element reflects Vancouver's iconic network of bridges, symbolizing seamless connections and the direct discounts available to you throughout the city.
