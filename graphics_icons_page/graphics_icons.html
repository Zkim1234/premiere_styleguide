Graphcis and Icons

Graphics

The graphics symbolizes the value our app offers to its users.

Premiere aims to find you the best deals and latest event in Vancouver.

Graphics are made with flat colours for simplicity and intuitiveness.

The simplicity of the graphics does not imply any specific metaphors. The graphics are used to communicate the utility of the app.

Navigation Bar

The icons in the navigation bar feature an outlined style with no drop shadows, and their colours are either white or black. Each icon has rounded edges, giving them a smooth and soft look. This design approach offers a modern, minimalist appearance that is easy on the eyes, helping to reduce cognitive overload. The outlined style ensures consistent visual weight across the icons, contributing to a clean, uniform, and open aesthetic throughout the interface.

Icons

All Icons

All icons are referenced from an Icon Pack and the rest are all customized.Icons are between 24px to 50px. Sizes depends on its usage.

White Icons

White icons are used to contrast backgrounds that are dark. Areas such as the page header or action buttons with dark backgrounds.

Black Icons

Black icons are used in areas with light backgrounds. These black icons can be found in most pages in light mode.

Images and Inspiration

Throughout the app, we prioritize inclusivity in the images we use. Premiere values its users by showcasing the best of Vancouver, offering competitive prices and hosting events that foster community connection.
